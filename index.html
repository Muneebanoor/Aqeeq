نفسِ مطمئنہ

میری نیندیں حرام کیا ہوں گی

This image captures the essence of hard work—an old man, content in his craft, earning his living with dignity. His satisfaction isn't in wealth or luxury, but in the peace that comes from knowing his earnings are halal, pure, and blessed. Such is the honor of those who toil with their hands, for their reward lies not just in the material world but in the tranquility of a satisfied soul—نفسِ مطمئنہ
