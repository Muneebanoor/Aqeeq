نفسِ مطمئنہ

میری نیندیں حرام کیا ہوں گی
میرے گھر رزق حلال آتا ہے

This image captures the essence of hard work—an old man, content in his craft, earning his living with dignity. His satisfaction isn't in wealth or luxury, but in the peace that comes from knowing his earnings are halal, pure, and blessed. Such is the honor of those who toil with their hands, for their reward lies not just in the material world but in the tranquility of a satisfied soul—نفسِ مطمئنہ

کوہ کندنی

ابھی تلک تو نہ کندن ہوئے نہ راکھ ہوئے
ہم اپنی آگ میں ہر روز جل کے دیکھتے ہیں

"This laborer, who melts iron and molds it into shape, could never change his own fate. But the strength of his resolve, is stronger than the iron itself. He is the one who could bend destiny with his hard work, yet all he holds is the skill of working with iron. The iron melts, but his dignity and hard work never will."
